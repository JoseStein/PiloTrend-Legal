# PiloTrend Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 19, 2026

PiloTrend is a mobile app for cryptocurrency market analysis, watchlists, and alerts on iOS and Android.

PiloTrend is provided for informational purposes only. It does **not** provide financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. You are responsible for your own trading and investment decisions.

## What PiloTrend Stores

PiloTrend currently does not require a user account.

The app stores some data locally on your device to support the app experience, including:

- your watchlist
- alert preferences
- local alert history
- app settings

This local data is used to make the app function properly on your device.

## Notifications

PiloTrend may request notification permission so it can send alert notifications to your device. You can allow or disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

## Market Data

PiloTrend fetches market and analysis data through its backend, which may use third-party market data providers and exchange data sources. Availability, freshness, and accuracy may depend on those external services.

## Personal Data

At this time, PiloTrend does not require account registration and does not intentionally collect profile information such as your name, password, or billing details.

PiloTrend does not sell your personal data.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future as the app changes. If the policy is updated, the latest version should be made available with an updated effective date.
